Author: Belinda Hurmence

Annoyed with her father for constantly bragging about his ancestors, an 11-year-old black girl learns some interesting truths when she is transported back to the year 1853. Ages 8-11. (Oct.) A pampered young African-American girl finds herself mysteriously transported back in time to the days of slavery.
